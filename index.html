About Us

Born from Nairobi’s vibrant street‑food culture, Kula Dawgs brings gourmet flair to the humble hotdog. We fuse locally sourced sausages with handcrafted toppings inspired by the city’s diverse flavors.

Every dawg is grilled to order, tucked in a pillowy bun, and finished with bold house sauces. Whether you’re pulling a late‑night study session or craving a quick office lunch, we’ve got a loaded hotdog with your name on it—delivered hot & fresh exclusively through Glovo and Uber Eats.
